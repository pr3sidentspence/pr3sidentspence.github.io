Shengyi CMT01N Motor

What little information I can find on this ebike motor.

I bought a Junction Rapid-E ebike from Canadian tire. There is very little technical or hacking information about this bike or its components available from either the bike manufacturer (Overlord Bikes Canada) or the motor maker Shengyi Motor of China. I'll document what I can find here.

Shengyi Motor's CMT01N page

Details

  • Mid Drive (Center Motor in Shengyi's parlance)
  • Rated power: 250W (mine almost always runs higher than this according to the display)
  • Max power: 500W (mine maxes out at around 530W according to the display)
  • Compatible displays: C965 (different connector layout and gender than Bafang)
